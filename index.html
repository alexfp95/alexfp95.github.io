About me

Hi, I'm Alexis Daniel Fuentes Pérez, I'm from Tenerife (Spain).

I'm a computer science engineer and video game programmer.
I studied a Degree in Computer Science Engineering at Universidad de La Laguna, and a Master's Degree in Design and Development of Video Games, at Universitat Oberta de Catalunya. Currently, I'm working as Game Programmer.

I have created this portfolio to publish my personal creations and projects.
